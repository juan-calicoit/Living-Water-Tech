6. Meta and WhatsApp Integration

Our services integrate with Meta platforms (including Facebook) and WhatsApp to enhance your experience and facilitate community engagement. When you use these integrations:

  • Information you share through our services may be shared with Meta or WhatsApp as necessary for the integration to function.
  • We may use Meta's Business Tools and WhatsApp's Business API to communicate with you and provide our services.
  • Your use of Meta and WhatsApp features is subject to their respective terms and privacy policies.

For more information about how Meta and WhatsApp handle your information, please review their privacy policies.
